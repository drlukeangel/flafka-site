About Flafka

We believe Confluent Cloud users deserve a world-class management experience.

Our Mission

Confluent Cloud is the best managed Kafka platform in the world. But its management console hasn't kept up with the complexity of modern streaming architectures. Teams deserve better visibility into schema dependencies, better cost controls, and better access management.

Flafka was born from the frustration of managing dozens of topics, hundreds of schemas, and unpredictable Confluent Cloud bills with tools that weren't designed for the job. We set out to build the management experience that Confluent Cloud operators actually need — one that shows you the blast radius before you break things, tells you what your Flink SQL will cost before you submit it, and gives you resource-level access control that auditors can verify.

Every feature in Flafka exists because a real team needed it and couldn't find it anywhere else.
