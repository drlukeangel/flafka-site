ROI Calculator FAQ

The ROI of Kafka management tools depends on team size and current debugging overhead. A team of 5 Flink SQL engineers spending 2 hours per week debugging errors typically sees a 17:1 ROI with Flafka, saving over $19,000 annually through reduced debugging time and cost visibility. Use the calculator above to estimate your team's specific ROI.

Kafka management ROI is calculated by estimating the debugging time saved (team size multiplied by hours saved per week, assuming a conservative 50% reduction), converting that to dollar savings using your average engineering hourly rate, and comparing the annual savings against Flafka's per-seat licensing cost ($19/seat/month). The formula: ROI = (annual_savings - flafka_cost) / flafka_cost * 100%.

Kafka debugging costs vary but typically consume 1-5 hours per engineer per week. For a team of 5 engineers at $75/hour, that is $375-$1,875 per week ($19,500-$97,500 annually) spent on debugging rather than building features. Flafka reduces this by 50% or more through real-time monitoring, per-statement cost attribution, and automated alerting.

CFU stands for Confluent Flink Unit, the billing unit for Flink SQL workloads on Confluent Cloud. Each running Flink statement consumes CFUs based on its compute requirements. Flafka tracks CFU consumption per statement in real time, helping teams monitor and control Flink SQL costs before they become budget surprises.
