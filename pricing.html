Frequently asked questions

Flafka is the developer tool — write Flink SQL, browse topics, manage schemas, set up data quality rules, and track compliance. Built for data engineers and Flink developers.

Flafka Ops is the operations platform — monitor clusters, manage connectors, handle incidents, track DR readiness. Built for SREs and platform engineers.

They are separate products with separate pricing. Same app, different workspace modes. Buy one or both.
No. Many teams start with just Flafka (the developer tool) and add Ops later when their operations needs grow. A developer only needs Flafka. An SRE might only need Ops. A platform lead benefits from both. You can add Ops at any time from your billing settings.
Flafka works with any Kafka cluster via SASL/PLAIN + TLS, including Confluent Cloud, AWS MSK, Apache Kafka, Redpanda, and Aiven. Confluent-specific features like Flink SQL IDE require a Confluent Cloud cluster.
When you sign up for Pro, you get a 14-day Team trial with full access to all Team features. No credit card required. After 14 days, you stay on your selected plan.
Annual billing saves you 2 months. Pro drops from $348/yr to $290/yr. Team drops from $2,388/yr to $1,990/yr. Ops Team also drops to $1,990/yr. Enterprise customers receive custom annual pricing. All annual plans are billed upfront.
Flafka Team includes 10 users and Ops Team includes 5 users. Additional users are $19/seat/month. For example, Flafka Team with 15 users is $199 + (5 x $19) = $294/month. Enterprise tiers include unlimited users.
